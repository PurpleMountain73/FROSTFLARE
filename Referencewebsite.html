Orcas

Orcas, also known as killer whales, are the largest members of the dolphin family. They are highly intelligent, social, and powerful predators found in oceans all over the world.

Did you know? Orcas live in pods and use complex vocalizations to communicate and hunt together.

Key Facts

  • Scientific Name: Orcinus orca
  • Diet: Carnivorous (fish, seals, even whales!)
  • Lifespan: Up to 90 years
  • Habitat: Oceans worldwide
Fun Fact: Each orca pod has its own unique dialect!
